**poki mu la mu mi li lon.**  

**mi kama jo e toki lili anu pilin la mi o pana e ona tawa lipu ni. kama la lipu ni li jo e mu mute mi.**  
**taso tenpo wan la mi pali e lipu ni. ken la tenpo mu li kama lon weka pi tenpo seli ni.**

This is poki mu. This roughly translates to "container of animal noises". It is a void I will throw text into. It is also I guess my personal website.

I think people who intend their *personal* website to be useful to others are extremely self-centered. This website is for me and (occasionally) some of my friends
it's not designed in a way that will make sense to a random person stumbling upon it, and most text you find here will likely be incomprehensible.

Then again, I recognize the value a personal website/blog can unintentionally provide to the most disconnected of visitors. Many a time have I caught myself reading through
the entirety of someone's blog, parasocially investing myself in their life, and maybe picking up a few facts or interests along the way. So while this website is intended
for me to look back on, anyone else who somehow finds themselves consuming most of the content of this website will likely learn a lot about me too.
